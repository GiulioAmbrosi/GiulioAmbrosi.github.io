If you are ready to discover an innovative nutritional approach to keeping healthy naturally, then you are in the right place.

My name is Giulio, a pharmacist, herbalist and nutritional consultant, ready to guide you to wellness through the healing power of nature and healthy food.

Who am I?

I have been passionate about herbal medicine and the natural sciences since I was a child, when thanks to my parents, who are both herbalists, I began to discover this fascinating and enthralling world.

After graduating from high school with a technical diploma, I decided to start a course of study that would allow me both to expand my knowledge of herbalism and to learn more about chemistry, which I developed an interest for in high school. I knew straight away the Faculty of Pharmacy was the way for me.

However, during my university career, divided between the ‘Carlo Bo’ University of Urbino and the University of Zagreb (Croatia), I discovered a common denominator to everything related to human health: nutrition. It was love at first sight.

After obtaining my Master's Degree in Pharmacy, with the thesis ‘The placebo effect in pain therapy’, I dedicated myself to working as a herbalist at the Demetra Herbalist Shop in Oderzo (TV). At the same time, I pursued my new passion with a degree course in Biology of Nutrition.

During my university years, in parallel with taking care of my diet and studying its psychological and biochemical effects, I also began to get passionate about the world of sport, discovering the indissoluble link that binds these two aspects of human health.

Using my experience with nutrition, as well as my experience competing in Powerlifting and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, I specialised in consulting in the field of sports nutrition and supplementation.

What do I offer?

In my consultancy, wellbeing is considered holistically, thanks to my multi-sectoral experience in phytotherapy, pharmaceuticals and nutrition.

My nutritional counselling focuses on helping you understand and adopt sustainable eating habits. We work together to develop a positive relationship with food and make conscious food choices that support your long-term health.

Through practical advice and in-depth information, I guide you towards a balanced diet that suits your lifestyle, preferences and health. In addition, I offer the possibility of augmenting your diet with personalised advice on the use of food supplements and medicinal plants. With an individualized approach, we can address your specific health needs in a natural and harmonious way.
